ANIMALS MAP PLACES

ELEPHANTS

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth. Gentle giants of jungle

MONKEY

Monkeys are intelligent primates known for their playful behavior and social structures.

INSECT-HOUSE

Insect houses are designed to provide shelter for various beneficial insects, promoting biodiversity.

LION

Lions are known as the kings of the jungle, recognized for their majestic manes and social pride structure.

KOALA

Koalas are marsupials native to Australia, known for their tree-dwelling habits and eucalyptus diet.

GIRAFFE

Giraffes are the tallest land animals, known for their long necks and unique coat patterns.

GORILLA

Gorillas are the largest primates and are known for their intelligence and social behavior.

GEMSBOK

Gemsbok are large antelopes with long, straight horns and a striking black and white facial pattern.

 TIGER

Tigers are the largest wild cats, known for their distinctive orange coat with black stripes.

 WARTHOG

Warthogs are wild pigs known for their distinctive tusks and rugged appearance.
